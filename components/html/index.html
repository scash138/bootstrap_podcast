About

Sterling is the host and has been a Dallas based musician for over 13 years. He is currently in Partaker and has previously played in Broadcast Sea. His love for podcasts has driven him to create his own podcast to talk about what he loves most, music.

Squirreling Podcast is dedicated to talking to local and national muscians about what they are up to and what they are listening to. Most of all he just wants to get to know other artists and talk about random shit.
